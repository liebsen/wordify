About
ReFocusing

Refocusing is fundamental to The Blue Ocean Company’s development initiatives. It is a powerful process that helps you to identify any limiting beliefs that may be getting in the way of your desire to do something different, and opens up a new path of action for you. The Refocus Tool is based on the technique of cognitive reframing (Aaron T Beck, 1960), the concept of paradigm shifts (Thomas Kuhn, 1962) and the methodology of philosopher Karim Benammar (2012).

Feel free to use the Refocus Tool as many times as you wish. Use it with your team, your division, your organisation. Or experience the full blown version in one of The Blue Ocean Company’s transformational programmes. Want to know more?
